## NOTES FOR AI IMAGE GENERATION

1. **Transparency is critical** — all sprites must have transparent PNG backgrounds, not white
2. **Exact pixel dimensions matter** — tModLoader expects precise sizes
3. **Spritesheets**: Enemy/NPC sprites are multiple frames stacked vertically with exactly 2 pixels of transparent padding between each frame
4. **Facing direction**: All NPCs and enemies face LEFT in their sprites
5. **Color palette**: Keep to 2-3 shading levels per color, avoid gradients or soft blending
6. **No anti-aliasing**: Clean, hard pixel edges only
7. **Scale**: These are very small sprites (12-80px). Details must be readable at actual size
