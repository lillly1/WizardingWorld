Wizarding World is a full progression adventure mod for Terraria, inspired by the Harry Potter stories and built for tModLoader 1.4.4+.

Start as a Hogwarts student, earn your first wand, follow a guided boss route, explore magical locations, and carry the fight through Horcruxes, Deathly Hallows, and Lord Voldemort as the true final boss.

Release highlights:
- 12 boss fights from Mountain Troll to Lord Voldemort
- 24 active wands, 9 spell schools, and Wand Mastery progression
- 78 enemies, 65 accessories, 8 armor sets, 5 mounts, 6 pets, and 19 potions
- 16 town NPCs with progression shops and quest handoffs
- Horcrux Hunt, Deathly Hallows, Dark Arts Corruption, Azkaban Despair, Quidditch, Hogsmeade, Diagon Alley, and Forbidden Forest systems
- Boss Compass and Wizard's Almanac guidance for the main route
- English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese localization

Public Release build: debug commands and QA-only test items are omitted.
