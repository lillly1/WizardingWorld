Enter the Wizarding World! This mod adds a complete magical experience to Terraria inspired by the world of Harry Potter.

Features:
- 20+ unique wands across 9 spell schools with Wand Mastery progression
- 12 boss fights from Mountain Troll to Lord Voldemort
- 78 enemies including Dementors, Acromantulas, Death Eaters, and Fantastic Beasts
- 8 armor sets (4 Hogwarts Houses, Dark Wizard, Dragon Scale, and more)
- 65 accessories including Deathly Hallows, Horcruxes, and House relics
- 16 town NPCs: Ollivander, Hagrid, Dumbledore, Dobby, and more
- 5 mounts (Nimbus, Firebolt, Hippogriff, Thestral, Nimbus 2001) and 6 pets
- 19 potions: Butterbeer, Felix Felicis, Polyjuice, Wolfsbane, and more
- Horcrux Hunt, Deathly Hallows questline, and Dark Arts Corruption systems
- Hogsmeade Village, Forbidden Forest biome, Quidditch, and Diagon Alley
- Full progression system tied to Terraria's vanilla bosses
- Three-language localization: English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese
