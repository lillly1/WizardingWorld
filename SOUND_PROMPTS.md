## INTEGRATION NOTES

To use custom music in tModLoader, boss files reference music via:
```csharp
if (!Main.dedServ)
    Music = MusicLoader.GetMusicSlot(Mod, "Assets/Music/BossName");
```

Sound effects are played via:
```csharp
SoundEngine.PlaySound(new SoundStyle("WizardingWorld/Assets/Sounds/Spells/SpellName"));
```

Replace the silent `.ogg` placeholders with real audio files of the same name. The mod will automatically use them.
