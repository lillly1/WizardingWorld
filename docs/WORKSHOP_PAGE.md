## Workshop Description

[h1]Wizarding World[/h1]

Wizarding World is a full progression adventure mod for Terraria, inspired by the Harry Potter stories and built for tModLoader 1.4.4+.

Begin as a Hogwarts student, receive your first wand, learn spells, follow a guided boss route, explore magical locations, and carry the story through Horcruxes, Deathly Hallows, and Lord Voldemort as the true final boss.

[h2]What is included?[/h2]

[list]
[*]12 boss fights, from Mountain Troll to Lord Voldemort
[*]24 active wands across 9 spell schools, with Wand Mastery progression
[*]78 enemies, 65 accessories, 8 armor sets, 5 mounts, 6 pets, and 19 potions
[*]16 town NPCs with progression shops, dialogue, and quest handoffs
[*]Horcrux Hunt, Deathly Hallows, Dark Arts Corruption, Azkaban Despair, Quidditch, Hogsmeade, Diagon Alley, and Forbidden Forest systems
[*]Boss Compass and Wizard's Almanac items to guide the main route
[*]English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese localization
[/list]

[h2]Progression[/h2]

The main route is built around Terraria progression and ends with Voldemort as the true final boss:

Mountain Troll -> Quirrell -> Basilisk -> Aragog -> Fluffy -> Horntail -> Umbridge -> Fenrir -> Bellatrix -> Barty Crouch Jr -> Dementor King -> Lord Voldemort

After Voldemort, the Deathly Hallows route resolves the final postgame reward state.

[h2]Release status[/h2]

This is the 1.0.0 release-candidate package. The primary single-player route has been smoke-tested from early game through Voldemort and the Deathly Hallows ending. The public Release build omits developer-only `/wwdebug` commands and QA-only test weapons.

Best tested: single-player progression.

Needs wider community testing: longer balance runs, multiplayer sync, fresh-machine installs, and edge-case world states.

[h2]Recommended first steps[/h2]

[list=1]
[*]Use a Life Crystal or defeat an early vanilla boss to trigger the Hogwarts Letter.
[*]Craft an Enchanting Table.
[*]Use the Boss Compass and Wizard's Almanac when you are unsure where to go next.
[*]Follow the magical boss route alongside normal Terraria progression.
[/list]
